What We Need To Serve You Successfully

From our experience, we know what makes a big difference. We encourage these things for yourself, and sort of require them if we work together:

1. We'd Need To Know Your Goals

Do you want to maximize occupancy, or price per night? Questions like this will matter a lot.

2. Linens: We'll Use Our Own

We believe that clean sheets and towels will make or break a guest's impression of a stay. We aggressively remove linens from rotation if they're discolored or worn, because we don't want a $20 towel to ruin a $400 booking.

We also like a good stockpile of linens, instead of the bare minimum. So we'll just use our own, and they'll be great.

3. Smart Locks: A Near-Must

The Schlage Encode smart lock is a near-must for successful hosting. Other models just prove to be finicky. Lockboxes are a bad experience, and unsafe because the code is reused, or the keys can be copied/lost.

Each guest will get a unique code for their booking, that will only be active during their booking.

4. Daily Maintenance

We are very frugal owners! We take our money very seriously and spend conservatively. But we feel very strongly that sometimes it costs more money to spend less.

It's important that the landscaping looks good, snow is addressed properly, maintenance items are fixed, and blankets/pillows/furniture looks good.

5. Smart Thermostats/Cooling Help

This can't be a "must" because sometimes a house can't support it. But if you can put in a smart thermostat, it can help a lot. We can automate it so that a guest walks in to a comfortable temperature when they arrive, and when a guest isn't there, you aren't heating/cooling an empty house.

6. Software Subscriptions

To run your house effectively, you will need some recurring software costs. You will own (and pay for) these platforms, but we will manage them for you:

  • $20/mo/property for a "Channel Manager" like Guesty
  • $20/mo/property for Pricing software, like Pricelabs
  • $5/mo/lock for automated lock codes
